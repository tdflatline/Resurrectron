Resurrectron by tdflatline

May all the twitternets be drowned by the din of inhuman laughter.

A project to create a twitter+foursquare AI based on a person's tweets, blog posts, mailinglist posts, irc logs, foursquare data, and other written material. The AI uses a supervised HMM trained on POS tag Ngrams to generate novel text, and TF-IDF to respond to messages and interesting tweets by people that the AI follows. It can also operate in a pure quotation engine mode without the HMM. Eventually it will also check in to foursquare and chat topically about its current location. For current capabilites see: http://github.com/tdflatline/Resurrectron/blob/master/settings.cfg For future plans, see: http://github.com/tdflatline/Resurrectron/blob/master/TODO.txt
